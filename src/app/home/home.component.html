About Us

At Simply Elegant by Diana, we're dedicated to creating seamless events based on the inspiration our clients have envisioned. We educate and guide our clients throughout the planning process to ensure a pleasurable experience as we bring their vision to life.

Whether you're dreaming of a fairytale wedding, a romantic elopement or an extravagant gala, we're here to carry the load so you can fully enjoy the experience of your event from begining to end.

Diana Dorsey

Founder & Certified Event Planner


Diana's passion for arts and entertainment was ignited at the age of 15, when she began choreographing waltz's for Quinceanera celebrations. She embraced her talent for curating events during the planning of her own wedding in 2010. Since then, she's demonstrated her abilities by oranizing, planning and creating breathtaking weddings, birthday parties and corporate events for almost 10 years.

Diana has a Bachelor's Degree in Business Administration and spent several years as an eduator in the Berkeley Unified School District. She believes education is important and although she's curated hundreds of events, she decided to take her experience even further, by becoming a Certified Event Planner.

Diana is recognized within the event industry for her calm and pleasant demeanor. Her goal is not only for clients to enjoy the planning and day-of experience but also, for all vendors involved to enjoy the process as well. Her "eagleye" attention to detail and willingness to go above and beyond, is what makes planning an event with Diana such a delight! It's no suprise, that clients choose to work with Diana for subsequent events, after their first experience with her.

Diana is known amongst friends and family to be a fun and loving person, always extending herself and helping others. If you don't see her hard at work, you'll find her dancing and entertaining, spending time with her beautiful family or traveling to an Island for a little relaxation.
